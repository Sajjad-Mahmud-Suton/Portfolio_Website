Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to questions you might have.

What services do you offer?

I offer web development, UI/UX design, mobile app development, and technical consulting services. Each project is tailored to meet your specific needs.

What is your typical project timeline?

Timelines vary based on project scope. A simple website takes 2-4 weeks, while complex web applications may take 2-3 months. I'll provide an estimate after our initial discussion.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes! I offer maintenance packages that include updates, security patches, performance optimization, and content updates to keep your site running smoothly.

What is your payment structure?

I typically work with a 50% upfront deposit and 50% upon completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments. I accept bank transfers and PayPal.

Can you work with my existing team?

Absolutely! I'm experienced in collaborating with in-house teams, other freelancers, and agencies. I'm flexible and can adapt to your workflow and communication preferences.

Are you available for freelance work?

Yes, I'm currently accepting new projects! Feel free to reach out with your project details, and I'll get back to you within 24-48 hours to discuss further.
